Welcome to ComplianceGPT

Financial institutions have to be on top of a plethora of laws and guidelines.
Sometimes, this means that while processes as stipulated in SOPs are being followed, those processes are outdated.
We believe that GenAI can be a powerful companion in quick QnA on specific laws, as a means of drafting new SOPs
and also as a way to perform gap-anayses on current SOPs. Therefore, we've developed ComplianceGPT.
It is still in development mode — but feel free to test it out!
